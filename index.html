About me

I received my Ph.D. degree in statistics in 2019 from the Department of Statistics, Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, under the supervision of Prof. David E. Tyler. Before that, I studied physics with a B.Sc. degree from Southeast University, Nanjing, China

Research

My research interests include

  • Multivariate statistics

  • Spatial statistics

  • Robust statistics

  • Time series analysis

  • High dimensional statistics
